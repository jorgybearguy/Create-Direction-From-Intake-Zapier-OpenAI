6) QUESTION SET


1. Can you describe a recent automation project you worked on and its impact on the business?


2. How do you approach identifying inefficiencies that can be automated?


3. What no-code/low-code tools are you most comfortable with, and why?
• Follow-Up: How do you decide which tool to use for a project?


4. Describe a time when you had to translate a vague problem into a clear solution.


5. How do you ensure the automations you build are scalable and maintainable?


6. What experience do you have with APIs and webhooks in your automation projects?
• Follow-Up: Can you provide a specific example?


7. How do you handle challenges when deploying an automation solution?


8. What strategies do you use to document your workflows effectively?


9. Tell me about a time you had to learn a new tool or technology quickly. How did you approach it?


10. How do you measure the success of an automation project?


11. Describe a situation where you had to collaborate with a team to implement an automation.


12. What role does creativity play in your approach to automation?


13. How do you prioritize which processes to automate first?


14. Can you discuss a time when an automation you implemented didn’t work as expected?


15. How do you stay updated on the latest trends in automation and AI?


16. What’s your approach to testing and validating automation solutions?


17. How do you handle feedback or resistance from stakeholders regarding automation changes?


18. Describe a project where you pushed the boundaries of what was possible with automation.


19. How do you balance technical novelty with business impact in your projects?


20. What excites you most about the potential of AI and automation in business?
